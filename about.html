About Us

Onwards and Upwards

Farm Fresh was growing faster then Loi could have ever imagined. To help expand his operations, he enlisted the help of his old friend Azmi Zainal as partner and brought in Khazanah National, the strategic investment arm of the Government of Malaysia, as an investor in the company. These collaborations laid the groundwork for the companies continued success..

Building A Legasy

At first, he found buyers hard to come by. After all, who would trust a Malaysian dairy brand? But one day, a major retailer took a chance on Farm Fresh.

Loi dream was becoming a reality.

Everyone could taste the difference

The word about Farm Fresh natural products soon spread, and before long our fresh milk and yoghurt products became available at supermarkets and hypermarkets all across Malaysia.
