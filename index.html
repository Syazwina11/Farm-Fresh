Welcome to Farm Fresh

Little Story

Ever since growing up in a small town in Perak, Loi Tuan Ee has held a deep respect for agriculture and a love of nature.Noticing that many Malaysian brands were selling products made from reconstituted or powdered milk, he decided to purchase a small plot of land in Johor and import 60 Holstein Jersey cows from Australia, setting up The Holstein Milk Company.

His dream? To produce fresh and pure dairy just as nature intended..
